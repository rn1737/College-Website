PLACEMENT

SRM institute OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY has concluded its placement drive for the batch 2022-23. The highest package and average package offered stood at INR 57 LPA and INR 7.58 LPA, respectively. Further, a total of 1,153 companies visited the campus for placement and made 8,609 offers to the students during placements 2023. A total of 3,250 high-paying offers were made to the students during SRM University placements 2023. Earlier, a total of 11 students of Allied Health Sciences of SRM Medical College Hospital and Research Centre have been placed at a reputed company in the USA. The salary package offered to these students during SRM placements 2022 stood at USD 72,000 per annum, which stood around INR 59 LPA. Earlier, a total of 1,050 companies visited the campus and placed more than 10,000 students in new-age job roles during SRM Institute of Science and Technology placements 2022. Further, SRM highest package offered in 2022 stood at INR 1 crore per annum. In addition, SRM University average package offered to the students of CS and IT departments stood at INR 9.5 LPA. The top recruiters of SRM IST in 2022 included Microsoft, Google, Amazon, HCL, IBM, TCS, etc.
